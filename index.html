BIO

Dr. Shauvik Roy Choudhary is the Founder of CheckDroid, where he works on building cutting edge testing and support tools for Android apps. He received his PhD in Computer Science from Georgia Tech and his work was focused on techniques for cross-platform testing and maintenance for web and mobile applications. Prior to his PhD, Shauvik received his MS in Information Security from Georgia Tech and his BE in Computer Engineering from Mumbai University. Over the years, he has held several development, testing and research positions at companies including, Google, Fujitsu Labs of America, Yahoo! Inc., IBM Research, Goldman Sachs and HSBC Software. For more information about Shauvik, visit his website at http://shauvik.com
